# Nomenclature (Alphabetized)

- Accused: player selected by Police Chief for trial.
- Circulating Currency: total money in the game (players x 100).
- Dead/Ghost: eliminated player who can observe full player state in real time but cannot participate.
- GDP: deprecated term for circulating currency.
- EFJ: Escape From Jail (replacement name for prior GOJFC term).
- Jailed: conviction transition state. If EFJ exists it auto-resolves to active; otherwise resolves to Dead/Ghost.
- Jury: randomly selected players who vote on trial outcome.
- Knife Hobo: lowest Mob rank, starts with 0 money.
- Merchant: independent player with solo win condition.
- Mob: team of hidden players attempting to eliminate Police.
- Mob Boss: highest Mob rank.
- Moderator: human authority, confirms deaths and resolves disputes, and controls game state.
- Police: team attempting to eliminate Mob with limited kills.
- Police Chief: highest Police rank; selects accused for trial.
- Round: sequence of Phase 1 information seeking, Phase 2 trial, then reset to Phase 1.
- Stash: moderator-provided pickup location for transferring items and money after trial resolution.
- Trial: formal resolution event after a murder.
