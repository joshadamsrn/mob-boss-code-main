# Items and Weapons

## Knives
- Represented by washable markers.
- A valid knife kill is a visible mark on skin.
- Players may not mark clothing.
- If marked, player must immediately admit death.
- Post-use money destination: none at item-use time. Any money/item transfer is resolved by trial/stash rules.

## Guns
- Three tiers: Tier I, Tier II, Tier III.
- All gun tiers are lethal unless otherwise specified by Moderator.
- Post-use money destination: none at item-use time unless a Bulletproof Vest is triggered.

## Bulletproof Vest
- One-time use defensive item.
- Protects wearer from one gunshot only.
- Does not protect against knives.
- If triggered, all players are notified that the target survived due to vest use and the vest is destroyed.
- No trial is started when a vest blocks a gunshot.
- The game round continues without interruption.
- Post-use money destination: vest value is transferred to the attacker.
- Visibility: vest use is publicly announced; attacker identity is hidden from global players.

## Escape From Jail (EFJ)
- One-time use item.
- May only be purchased before the first trial of the game.
- Cannot be purchased by jailed players.
- No items may be used during a trial by player action.
- If the accused is voted Guilty and owns EFJ, it is auto-used immediately after conviction by server resolution.
- When auto-used, the player remains in the game, no stash/resource transfer occurs, and no role reveal occurs.
- If the accused is voted Guilty and does not own EFJ, they are eliminated immediately.
- Post-use money destination: EFJ purchase value goes to a random Police player (Chief/Deputy/Detective all eligible).
- Visibility: recipient gets private "accepted a bribe" notification.

Money effects (global rule):
- Money cannot leave the game economy.
- Every item with a money effect must define an explicit recipient rule.

## Merchant Sales
- Merchants can sell to anyone.
- Merchants may adjust prices relative to shop price.
- Only Merchants can buy from central supply.

## Inventory
- Unlimited items may be carried.

## Purchase Windows
- Purchases are allowed during gameplay but not during trials.
