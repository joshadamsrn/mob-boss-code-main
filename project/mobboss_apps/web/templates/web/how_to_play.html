How to Play Mob Boss

A game of secrecy, strategy, money, and survival.

You have a hidden role, a special ability, and a win condition.

Factions

  • Police: eliminate all Mob players.
  • Mob: eliminate all Police players.
  • Merchants: reach your money goal.

Mob players also share a secret word they can use carefully to identify each other.

Keep your role secret. If other players figure out who you are, you may become a target.

How It Works

  • A moderator runs the game and makes final decisions.
  • Players talk, trade, use abilities, and pursue their objective.
  • If a murder happens, the game pauses for a trial.
  • Only one player can be eliminated at a time: 1 murder, 1 trial.
  • Players discuss, accuse a suspect, and a verdict is reached.
  • Then normal gameplay continues.

Money and Elimination

  • Players can earn money, trade items, and use abilities.
  • Weapons must be found or bought during the game.
  • If you are murdered, your killer takes your money and items.
  • If you are eliminated in a trial, your resources go to the winning side of that trial.

Tips

Learn your objective.

Use your ability wisely.

Watch other players closely.

Trust carefully.

Follow the moderator’s instructions.

How to Win

  • Police: eliminate all Mob.
  • Mob: eliminate all Police.
  • Merchants: reach your money goal.
